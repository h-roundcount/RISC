Methods
Reference

We examined the list of hundreds of indicator variables provided in the VDEM dataset and selected the eighteen that were deemed most relevant to the aims of our analysis. These were all continuous variables, with the majority on an interval between 0 and 1. In order to aggregate these indicators into one overall score, we determined the meaning of each score. We weighted all indicators equally, and added all of those for which a high score was a good thing, and subtracted from them all of those for which a high score was a bad thing. We then scale the score down, dividing it by 100 in order to get a decimal between -1 and 1, with higher numbers being better overall country scores. The chosen variables were as followed:

  • v2xcl_rol (equality before the law and individual liberty index) 
  • v2xeg_eqprotec(equal protection index) 
  • v2xeg_eqaccess (equal access index) 
  • v2xeg_eqdr (eqal distribution of resources index)
  • v2pepwrgen (power distribution by gender) 
  • v2pepwrses (power distribution by socioeconomic position) 
  • v2clgencl (gender equality in respect for civil liberties)
  • v2caviol (political violence) 
  • v2caassemb (freedom of peaceful assembly) 
  • v2cainsaut (institutional autonomy) 
  • v2cacamps (political polarization) 
  • v2cagenmob(mass mobilization) 
  • v2x_gencl (women civil liberties index) 
  • v2smgovdom (government dissemination of false information domestic) 
  • v2smgovab (government dissemination of false information abroad) 
  • v2smgovsmcenprc(government social media censorship in practice) 
  • v2smpolhate (political party hate speech) 
  • v2xel_frefair(clean election index ordinal) 

The final equation is given by: 

agg = sum((v2xcl_rol+v2xeg_eqprotec+v2xeg_eqaccess+v2xeg_eqdr+v2pepwrgen+v2pepwrses+v2clgencl- v2caviol+v2caassemb+v2cainsaut-v2cacamps-v2cagenmob+v2x_gencl+v2smgovdom+v2smgovab+v2smgovsmcenprc+ v2smpolhate+v2xel_frefair)/100))

Metrics Used:

  • Electoral Democracy Index(v2x_polyarchy): an interval from 0,1 measuring the strength of a country's electoral democracy
  • Democratization Episode: A period of substantial and sustained increases on V-Dem’s Electoral Democracy Index (EDI)
  • Autocratization Episode:  A period of substantial and sustained decreases on V-Dem’s Electoral Democracy Index (EDI)
  • Democratization Episode Outcome, Aggregate (dem_ep outcome_agg): 
    • 0– There is no democratization episode during this year.
    • 1– Democratic transition. The episode resulted in a change from autocracy to democracy on the RoW measure followed by a democratic founding election. Applies to entire episode, even if subsequent democratic deepening occurred.
    • 2– No democratic transition. The episode did not result in a change from autocracy to democracy on the RoW measure; or it did result in a change between democracy and autocracy on the RoW measure, but the political unit did not hold a democratic founding election before reverting to autocracy.
    • 3– Deepened democracy. The episode resulted in further liberalization or democratization of a political unit that was already classified as democracy in the pre-episode year.
    • 4– Uncertain. The outcome of the episode is uncertain because it has the potential for a regime change (dem ep prch==1) but is ongoing in  the final observation year of the dataset or before a gap in coding is encountered.
